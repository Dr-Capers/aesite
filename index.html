Thumb War

THUMB-WAR is a real-time mobile arcade battler where players duel 1-on-1 in short, high-intensity rounds using touch-based gestures. Players charge, swipe, block, and unleash their character’s unique abilities to outsmart opponents in a battle of timing and rhythm.

The game sits at the intersection of skill, style, and street competition, blending kinetic touchscreen gameplay with collectible character progression, daily rewards, and social bragging rights.
